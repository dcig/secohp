Sugar Eco-System(seco)

The essential idea.

The app is designed to analyze your glucose trends over time and look for patterns by providing insights on insulin timing, sleep and other health data. By entering your desired blood sugar ranges that SugarEco needs for baseline tracking, you’ll create the parameters for how seco creates relationships and identifies patterns among variables that humans have a hard time maintaining.

Data Inputs

What are contributing to the insights?

You :)

At minimum you’ll have to enter your insulin dosages. Most importantly is the timing of your basal dose! We are trying to help establish a foundation to build upon for long-term managment.

Dexcom

Synching your Dexcom account to the seco allows realtime BGs to be saved. This provides seco with the fundamental information for it to create relationships and event tracking.

Fitbit

The core and most unique part of the system. The watch serves as a tool to enter dosage and carb amounts while informing seco of sleep, fitness and heart rate information.

Layman's Terms

How seco is interacting with your data differently.
seco collects
Seco collects blood sugar “events” and labels them as either “good” or “bad”. Then it collects sleep and fitness data that correspondes to the bg “events” establishing relationships between variables.
seco categorizes
The failed tests will be displayed and reasons why seco thinks it failed. All the passing tests are now defined as “potentials”, or newly discovered patterns and insights that have played a role in blood sugar fluctuations.
seco creates tests
The tests will indicate whether or not the recent categorizations were valid and the upcoming recursive tests are valid as well. Finally, the tests get compared and evaluated for any patterns.
seco asks questions
The tests and their results get questioned to see what happens when specific variables get modified. The resulting sugar trends are then compared against the original set of questions to see the difference.
seco runs tests and collects
Based on the previous differences, seco re-runs tests expecting to see certain results. There will be a difference in the tests that passed and tests that failed. That value will determine how accurate seco is identifying anamolies in your sugar trends.

Eco-System

What the eco-system is envisioned to become

The eco-system is envisioned to have 4 key parts as the foundation. The goal is cover each level of care an individual would like to have while having the least amount of maintance as possible.

seco
The blood sugar trend identifier app.
pump
First low-cost/maint pump.
meter
All-in-one low-cost meter.
insulin
Low-cost fast-acting insulin.

About me and this idea

My dilemma turned into my therapy.

I was dissatisfied with what was available for diabetics and the cost of these products. Let alone the waste that I create from them just to stay alive. 2017-2018 was altering my perception. I experienced two near life death experiences, DKA, partial blindness for a month after t1d diagnosis and having to close my previous business, I really started to re-evaluate time. My time, and what I have done with it. I think that it takes time, patience and vision to build something that can achieve sustainable change. I play the long game and will build the eco-system over years of constant iteration.
